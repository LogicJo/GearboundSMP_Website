The World Beyond

This server stays true to Minecraft's original vision. No gameplay-altering restrictions stand between you and the world. The story of Gearbound SMP is written entirely by its players.

Whether you choose to build hidden bases, create massive infrastructure, dominate the server through force, or disappear into the wilderness, every decision is yours to make.

This Server has a Mod installed that brings the Farlands back to Minecraft.

Once you travel beyond 12 million blocks from spawn, reality begins to break apart.

Massive walls of terrain stretch into the horizon. Mountains rise in impossible formations. Chunks generate into chaotic structures unlike anything found in modern Minecraft.
